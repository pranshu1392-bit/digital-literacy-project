## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Verify all payment requests before approving them
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Check your bank statements regularly
- Do not download applications from unknown sources

# UPI Safety Tips
- Do not approve a “collect request” unless you trust the person
- You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money
